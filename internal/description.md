# App Store Description — VivaDicta v2.0.0
**Last updated:** 2026-03-05

---

Dictate Anywhere You Type

Type 4x faster with your voice. Whether you're walking, commuting, or capturing ideas on the go — just speak and let AI transform your words into polished, ready-to-send text. Completely free.

WORKS EVERYWHERE YOU TYPE

One keyboard for all your apps. Dictate into Messages, WhatsApp, Email, Notion, or anywhere else — no app switching required. Your voice becomes text instantly. Swipe to switch between modes right from the keyboard.

AI PRESETS

Process your text with 40+ built-in AI presets across categories:
• Rewrite — Regular, Coding, Simplify, Proofreading
• Style — Professional, Casual, Short, Expand
• Communication — Email, Chat
• Summarize — Summary, Action Points, Key Points, Mind Map
• Social Media — Instagram, LinkedIn, X, YouTube, and more
• Writing — Journal, Blog, Philosophical
• Learn & Study — Study Notes, Definitions, Quiz
• Translate — Speak in one language, get text in another. Dictate in English, receive output in Spanish — or any language
• Assistant — Ask a question or give an instruction. Fact-check, explain, reformat, change tone — just say what you need

Each result is saved as a variation — compare different outputs side by side. Create your own custom presets and mark favorites for quick access.

COMPLETELY FREE, NO EXCEPTIONS

Every feature available to everyone. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no limits. Download on-device AI models, use the custom keyboard, create unlimited transcriptions — all without spending a penny.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Your voice never leaves your iPhone:
• All transcription runs on your device
• Zero telemetry or tracking
• No accounts or sign-ups required
• No data collection, period
• Works in airplane mode

Cloud AI (OpenAI, ElevenLabs, Groq, Cerebras, Gemini, and more) is available if you want it — but it's completely optional and always under your control.

AI THAT PROCESSES YOUR SPEECH

Raw transcriptions become polished text automatically. AI removes filler words, fixes punctuation, and formats everything perfectly. See the original alongside the processed version — one tap to copy either.

VIVA MODES

Switch between AI-powered modes for different contexts. Each mode remembers its own AI processing preset, provider, and model. Set a Translate preset as your default and every recording is automatically translated.

CLIPBOARD & SELECTED TEXT CONTEXT

AI processing can use your clipboard or selected text as additional context. For example, copy a message you received, then dictate your reply — AI knows what you're responding to. Configurable per mode.

NOW ON iPHONE AND MAC

VivaDicta works across iPhone and Mac with full iCloud sync. Transcriptions, AI presets, custom dictionary, and API keys sync seamlessly between all your devices.
